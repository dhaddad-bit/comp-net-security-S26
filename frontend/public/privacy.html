Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Social Scheduler is a university capstone project. This Privacy Policy explains what information we access and how we use it.

Information We Access

If you connect your Google account, we request read-only access to your Google Calendar events.

How We Use Data

We use calendar event information only to compute shared group availability and help users coordinate meeting times.

Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your Google Calendar data for advertising.

Data Retention

We retain only the minimum information needed to support application features and improve reliability.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the project team through your university course channels.
