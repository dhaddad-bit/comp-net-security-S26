Because you are requesting Calendar data, Google classifies this as a Restricted or Sensitive Scope. This means before you can go public, your app must pass a Google Trust & Safety review, requiring a clear Privacy Policy, secure data handling (Ch 1. Confidentiality), and proper OAuth token storage (Ch 2. Authentication).
